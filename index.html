Privacy Policy

Flowing Palette · Last updated: June 1, 2026

Flowing Palette ("the app", "we", "us") is a single-player color puzzle game. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. Flowing Palette does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no login, and makes no network requests of its own.

Data stored on your device

To make the game work, the app saves the following locally on your device only (using the operating system's standard on-device storage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party:

  • Your level progress (completed levels, perfect-solve stars).
  • Bonus moves granted on individual levels.
  • Your preferences (sound, vibration, colorblind mode).

You can erase all of this at any time from Settings → Reset progress, or by deleting the app.

Third parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not use cookies or any cross-app/website tracking.

Permissions

The app requests no special permissions (no camera, no microphone, no location, no contacts, no network access for data collection). Sound effects are played from files bundled inside the app.

Children

Flowing Palette does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for all ages and contains no data collection, ads, or external links.

Platform-level data

Apple and Google may collect limited technical information (such as crash logs or aggregate download statistics) at the operating-system / app-store level, governed by their own privacy policies. We do not receive any personal data from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
